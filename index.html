Welcome to Dalkeith Guest House, Keswick

We welcome you to the family run Dalkeith Guest House – a beautiful stone-built Lakeland Bed & Breakfast Guest House set in the Cumbrian market town of Keswick in the heart of the Lake District National Park. The atmosphere here is of a small friendly country guest house, rather than a formal hotel. After all, Dalkeith is our home, and you are our guests!The house is ideally situated in a quiet location only five minutes walk into the town centre of Keswick, with views towards the Skiddaw Fells to the North.

It is an ideal base for exploring the Lakeland Fells – the Lake District is ideal for both serious walkers and strollers. Nearby are the lakes of Derwentwater and Bassenthwaite and the whole town is surrounded by magnificent mountains.

Mellow Borrowdale slate, traditional architecture built in 1878, just a few minutes walk from the River Greta & Fitz Park.

We hope you enjoy visiting our web site, and find the information you need about our friendly Keswick guest house accommodation and the local Keswick area. However, if you have any queries, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

A warm welcome awaits all our guests.
