Dalkeith Guest House: Breakfast

A choice of breakfasts, including a delicious traditional full English breakfast, is served every morning in our Breakfast Room between 8am and 9am.

Early breakfast can be arranged if required. Just let us know in advance.

Any special dietary requirements, likes or dislikes are happily catered for by arrangement. Please don’t be afraid to ask.

Our breakfast ingredients are locally sourced wherever possible. Cocklakes Farm, Troutbeck supply our handmade Cumberland sausage and
dry cured bacon and our free range eggs come from Scales Farm in Embleton.

We serve tea and coffee in our breakfast room and bedrooms.

Flasks can be filled on request.
